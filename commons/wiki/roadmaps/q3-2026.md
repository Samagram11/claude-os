# Q3 2026 Roadmap — Draft

## Summary
Q3 must protect $1M in at-risk ARR from [[halcyon-health]] while closing a feature gap that also threatens [[deals/halcyon-health|DataBridge Co]]'s $320K renewal. A working Smart Alerts prototype already exists and can ship fast — the top priority is connecting engineering's ready-to-go work to the customers who need it most.

---

## Priority 1: Smart Alerts (Weeks 1–4)
**Why:** [[halcyon-health]] ($1M ARR, renewal May 5 — cancellation window closes today) explicitly requested rules-based alerting on Apr 12, with competitor demos already underway; a working prototype in `feature/smart-alerts` with 47 passing tests just needs product sign-off to ship.
**Effort:** [[james-wright]]'s prototype is feature-complete (threshold alerts, multi-channel delivery, dashboard UI). Needs [[lin-zhang]] merge approval, beta onboarding with Halcyon, and hardening for production.
**Accounts impacted:** [[halcyon-health]] (critical), plus multiple accounts from Q1 survey who cited alerting as #1 missing feature.

---

## Priority 2: Dashboard Embedding (Weeks 3–8)
**Why:** [[deals/halcyon-health|DataBridge Co]] ($320K renewal) is actively evaluating Tableau specifically because of this gap, and TechFlow Inc has had this request on the roadmap since Nov 2025.
**Effort:** New build — needs scoping by [[lin-zhang]] and [[james-wright]]. Iframe-based embed with auth passthrough. Medium complexity; no prototype exists yet.
**Accounts impacted:** DataBridge Co ($320K at risk), TechFlow Inc ($320K ARR).

---

## Priority 3: Custom Metrics Builder (Weeks 7–13)
**Why:** Nexus Labs submitted this request in Jan 2026 and it represents a key expansion opportunity; completing it strengthens Meridian's positioning against Tableau and Looker in the enterprise segment.
**Effort:** Needs formal scoping from [[lin-zhang]]; no prototype exists. Moderate-to-high complexity.
**Accounts impacted:** Nexus Labs (primary), broader enterprise segment.

- **New data connectors:** No active renewal risk tied to connector gaps; existing 20+ integrations cover current customer needs.
