# Q3 2026 Roadmap — Draft

## Summary
Q3 is a retention quarter first. Two active at-risk accounts — Halcyon Health ($1M ARR) and DataBridge Co — map directly to the top two features on this roadmap; losing either would trigger board-level concern. We ship what's closest to done and highest in revenue impact, in that order.

---

## Priority 1: Smart Alerts / Rules-based Alerting (Weeks 1–4)
**Why:** The #1 most-requested enterprise feature and the fastest path to retaining Halcyon Health ($1M ARR, 12.5% of revenue) — whose cancellation window closes today (Apr 12) and whose renewal is due May 5. Halcyon runs dashboards across 3 hospital sites with direct alerting use cases. A working demo this week is the strongest available retention lever.
**Effort:** Low — prototype is feature-complete in `feature/smart-alerts` (James Wright, Mar 2026 hackathon; 47 tests passing, 0 failing; architecture approved by Lin Zhang). Needs product sign-off to merge, a customer beta arranged with Halcyon, and a sales briefing that has not yet happened.
**Accounts:** Halcyon Health ($1M ARR); broad enterprise segment per Q1 feedback survey.

---

## Priority 2: Dashboard Embedding (iframe support) (Weeks 5–10)
**Why:** DataBridge Co is actively evaluating Tableau specifically because it offers native dashboard embedding for their client portal — a gap that, if unaddressed, repeats the 2025 Looker loss pattern (won then; must win again).
**Effort:** Medium — no prototype exists; net-new build. Full team of 5 engineers (Lin Zhang's org) available after Smart Alerts ships. Dashboard embedding is already on the roadmap; this accelerates it.
**Accounts:** DataBridge Co (ARR undisclosed, Medium churn risk); also addresses Halcyon Health's known product request.

---

## Priority 3: Scheduled Report Delivery (Weeks 9–13)
**Why:** The #2 most-requested feature on the Meridian Platform, with partial groundwork already laid in v3.1; ships to broad enterprise demand without a specific churn trigger.
**Effort:** Medium — no prototype; builds on v3.1 delivery infrastructure. Can overlap with embedding work in Weeks 9–10.
**Accounts:** Halcyon Health (known request); broad enterprise customer base (~50 accounts).

---

## What We're Not Doing
- **Custom Metrics Builder** — #4 on the feature request list; no urgency signal and no prototype. Deferred to Q4.
- **New customer verticals / integrations** — Q3 engineering capacity is fully committed to retention-critical features; new connector work stays in backlog.
